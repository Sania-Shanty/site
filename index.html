About Us

The Computer Society of India (CSI) Student Chapter at SAU is a vibrant hub dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and learning among tech enthusiasts. We focus on providing hands-on experience and building a strong community.

Our Mission

Empowering students through practical projects, mentorship, and a collaborative learning environment.

Our Vision

To cultivate a dynamic community of tech makers who create impactful solutions and drive technological advancement.
